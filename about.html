ARTIST - PORTRAITS & PAINTINGS

I was introduced to art at a very young age. I specialise in Semi Realistic Figurative, Abstract Paintings, Charcoal & Pencil Portraits. I have always loved to create Charcoal & Pencil Portraits but my passion grew stronger after I came across works of artists like Kathe Kollwitz & Robert Lango. Portraits are inherently a celebration of life. Charcoal is a great medium for studying the effect of light & shadow across a subjects face.

​

My work tends to focus on Human Life & Nature. None of this was intentional, rather it all developed & evolved over time. I have never liked to explain any of my work. Hopefully my work can speak for itself. I believe the viewer should concentrate on their emotional response to a particular painting & not on what the artist had in mind while creating it.

​

Working as an Art Restorer added to my experience. As a part of my work, I undertook the restoration of Oils, Watercolors, Papers, Manuscripts, Animal Skin, Metal Objects, coins, Kadathas & Murals. I have a keen interest in & an eye for Photography as well.
